Pesto pizza

An alternative to your regular pizza.

Pairs with Cabernet Sauvignon, Merlot, Pinot Noir and Sauvignon Blanc.

Ingredients

  • 30 cm pre-baked pizza crust
  • 50g pesto
  • 1 ripe tomato, chopped
  • 1 green bell pepper, chopped
  • 50g olives, chopped
  • half of a small red onion, chopped
  • 100g crumbled feta cheese
  • 100g mushrooms

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 230 degrees C
  2. Spread pesto on pizza crust. Top with tomatoes, bell peppers, olives, red onions, mushrooms and feta cheese.
  3. Bake for 10 minutes, or until cheese is melted and bronwed.
